# Sales Playbook

An [Agent Skill](https://skills.sh) for the **full sales cycle** — from first cold touch to closed deal.

While most sales skills cover only cold email, this playbook gives AI agents expert knowledge across **outreach, objection handling, closing, follow-up, persuasion psychology, and 6 proven sales methodologies**.

Built for B2B SaaS, digital products, and service businesses.

## What's Inside

### Outreach (3 channels, 10+ frameworks)
[cold-outreach.md](sales-playbook/references/cold-outreach.md) — Email frameworks (PAS, AIDA, BAB, QVC, PPP, 3C's, Mouse Trap, Star-Story-Solution, SCQ), WhatsApp templates, cold call scripts, subject line optimization with data, 4-level personalization system, benchmarks, and cultural calibration.

### Objection Handling (7 objections, 20+ responses)
[objections.md](sales-playbook/references/objections.md) — "Too expensive," "Not interested," "Already use a competitor," "Need to think about it," "No budget," "Send me info," "No time." Each with multiple techniques from Voss, Tracy, Sandler, and Cardone.

### Closing & Follow-Up (10+ techniques, 30-day sequence)
[closing-and-followup.md](sales-playbook/references/closing-and-followup.md) — Assumptive, Summary, Trial, Alternative, 1-10, Sharp Angle, Cost of Inaction, and more. Plus a data-backed multi-channel follow-up cadence with breakup email templates.

### Sales Methodologies (6 frameworks)
[methodologies.md](sales-playbook/references/methodologies.md) — SPIN Selling, The Challenger Sale, Sandler Selling System, Straight Line Selling, Gap Selling, and 10X Rule. With a decision matrix for when to use each.

### Persuasion Psychology
[persuasion.md](sales-playbook/references/persuasion.md) — Cialdini's 7 principles, Chris Voss tactical empathy (mirroring, labeling, calibrated questions, accusation audit), storytelling frameworks, loss aversion, trust formula, and buyer type classification.

### Seller Mindset
[mindset.md](sales-playbook/references/mindset.md) — Self-concept theory, Three Tens of Certainty, state management, 10X Rule, resilience against rejection, and the 40/30/20/10 modern sales model.
